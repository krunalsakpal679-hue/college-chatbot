# Drs. Kiran and Pallavi Patel Global University (KPGU), Vadodara

## University Overview
- **Establishment**: Founded in 2004.
- **Approvals**: AICTE, UGC, PCI, and NBA.
- **Location**: Vadodara, Gujarat.
- **Website**: https://kpgu.ac.in

## Academic Programs
KPGU offers over 36 undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), diploma, and Ph.D. programs across various faculties.

### Engineering (Krishna School of Technology)
- **Courses (B.Tech)**: Computer Engineering (CSE), Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering (EE), Electronics & Communication Engineering (BEC/ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), and Information Technology (IT).
- **Courses (M.Tech)**: Data Science, Advanced Manufacturing Systems.
- **Eligibility**: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (minimum 45% marks, 40% for reserved categories).
- **Admission**: Based on JEE Main or GUJCET scores followed by ACPC counseling.
- **Fees for B.Tech/CSE**: Approximately ₹3.99 Lakhs - ₹4.94 Lakhs for the total 4-year duration.

### Pharmacy (Krishna School of Pharmacy)
- **Courses**: B.Pharm, M.Pharm.
- **Admission**: GUJCET or university entrance exams are considered.

### Physiotherapy (Krishna School of Physiotherapy)
- **Courses**: Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT), Master of Physiotherapy (MPT).
- **Duration (BPT)**: 4.5 years (including internship).

### Management & Applications (Krishna School of Management)
- **Courses**: BBA, BCA, MBA.
- **MBA Admission**: Requires valid CAT, MAT, XAT, or CMAT scores + 50% aggregate in graduation.

### Science (Krishna School of Science)
- **Courses (B.Sc)**: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Microbiology, and Biotechnology.
- **Environment**: High individualized support and encouraging research environment.

### Allied Health & Medicine
- **BAMS**: Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery.
- **Nursing**: B.Sc. Nursing and GNM.

## Fee Structure (Estimated)
- **B.Tech**: ₹3.99 Lakhs - ₹4.94 Lakhs (Total 4-year duration).
- **BPT**: Approx ₹5 Lakhs (Total 4-year duration).
- **B.Sc**: Approx ₹90,000 (Total 3-year duration).
- **MBA**: Approx ₹1.33 Lakhs (2-year program).
- **Diploma (After 10th)**: Approx ₹1.62 Lakhs (Total including deposits).
- **General Range**: Fees vary from ₹81,000 to ₹27.5 Lakhs depending on program and duration.

## Infrastructure & Campus
- **Smart Classrooms**: ICT-enabled with modern projectors, air-conditioned options.
- **Laboratories**: High-tech labs and research workshops for all major disciplines.
- **Hostels**: Separate hostels for boys and girls. Facilities include Furnished AC/Non-AC rooms (2/3-bed), attached baths, dining hall, AV lounge, and medical center.
- **Campus Life**: Large green campus, Wi-Fi connectivity, cafeteria with quality food, sports playgrounds, and gymnasium.

## Faculty
KPGU is known for highly qualified faculty experts who promote critical thinking and individualized support for students across all schools.
