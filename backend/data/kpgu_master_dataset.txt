### 5. ADMISSION PROCESS & ELIGIBILITY
- **Engineering/Pharmacy**: Admissions via **ACPC** (Admission Committee for Professional Courses), Gujarat.
- **Entrance Exams**:
  - **B.Tech**: GUJCET or JEE Main.
  - **B.Pharma**: GUJCET or NEET.
  - **B.A.M.S.**: NEET is mandatory.
  - **M.B.A.**: CMAT / CAT / MAT scores.
  - **Ph.D.**: Entrance Test (KPGU-PET) + Interview.
- **Eligibility**:
  - UG: Minimum 45% (General) / 40% (SC/ST/OBC) in 12th Standard with relevant subjects (PCM/PCB).
  - PG: Graduation in relevant field with 50%+.
